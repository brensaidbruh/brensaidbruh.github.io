About The Brew Notebook

The Brew Notebook was created as a space to document simple yet refined coffee recipes inspired by three years of part-time barista experience.

Balancing full-time academic commitments while working in a café environment provided practical insights into flavor balance, brewing precision and customer preferences.

This platform aims to simplify café-style drinks for home brewers, making quality coffee accessible and enjoyable for everyday routines.
