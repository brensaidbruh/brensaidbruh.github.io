Classic Brews

Espresso

Ingredients

  • 18g finely ground coffee

Steps

  1. Tamp coffee grounds evenly.
  2. Extract for 25–30 seconds.
  3. Serve immediately.

Americano (black coffee)

Ingredients

  • 1 double espresso (40g)
  • Hot water

Steps

  1. Pull espresso shot.
  2. Add 150ml hot water.
  3. Serve warm.

Coffee latte (white coffe)

Ingredients

  • 1 double espresso (40g)
  • 200ml steamed milk

Steps

  1. Prepare espresso.
  2. Steam milk until silky.
  3. Pour milk over espresso.

Mocha

Ingredients

  • 1 double espresso (40g)
  • 10g cacao powder
  • 15g sugar syrup
  • 200ml steamed milk

Steps

  1. Mix cacao powder and sugar with espresso.
  2. Add steamed milk.
  3. Top with cocoa powder.

Cold Brew

Ingredients

  • 100g coarse coffee grounds
  • 1000g cold water

Steps

  1. Mix coffee with water.
  2. Steep 12–18 hours in fridge.
  3. Strain and serve over ice.

Dirty Latte

Ingredients

  • Cold milk
  • 1 double espresso (40g)

Steps

  1. Fill cold glass with cold milk.
  2. Slowly extract espresso over milk.
  3. Serve layered.

⭐ Special Brew

Hojicha Latte

Ingredients

  • 5g hojicha powder
  • 150ml milk
  • Sweetener (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk hojicha and sweetener with warm water.
  2. Add steamed milk.

Affogato

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 single shot espresso (20g)

Steps

  1. Place ice cream in cup.
  2. Pour hot espresso over.
  3. Serve immediately.

Yuzu Sparkling Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 double espresso (40g)
  • Yuzu syrup
  • Sparkling water
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Fill glass with ice.
  2. Add yuzu syrup and sparkling water.
  3. Top with espresso.
