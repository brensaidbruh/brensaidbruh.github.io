☕ Classic Brews

Espresso

Ingredients: 18g finely ground coffee

Extraction: 25–30 seconds

Americano (Black Coffee)

Hot: Double espresso + 150ml hot water

Ice: Double espresso + cold water + ice

Latte (White Coffee)

Hot: Espresso + 200ml steamed milk

Ice: Espresso + cold milk + ice

Mocha

Hot: Espresso + cacao + steamed milk

Ice: Espresso + chocolate syrup + cold milk + ice

Cold Brew

100g coarse coffee + 1000g water

Steep 12–18 hours, strain and serve over ice.

Dirty Latte

Cold milk + freshly extracted espresso layered on top.

⭐ Special Brews

Hojicha Latte

Hot: Hojicha powder + steamed milk

Ice: Hojicha whisked + cold milk + ice

Affogato

Vanilla ice cream topped with hot espresso.

Yuzu Sparkling Coffee

Yuzu syrup + sparkling water + espresso over ice.
