Recipes

Learn coffee recipes crafted with barista experience and practical industry guidance for everyday brewing.

☕ Classic Brews

Espresso

Ingredients: 18g finely ground coffee

Extraction: 25–30 seconds

Americano (Black Coffee)

Hot: Double espresso + 150ml hot water

Ice: Double espresso + 120ml cold water + ice

Latte (White Coffee)

Hot: Espresso + 200ml steamed milk

Ice: Espresso + 120mlcold milk + ice

Mocha

Hot: Espresso + 10g cacao + 200ml steamed milk

Ice: (Espresso + 10g cacao) + 120ml cold milk + ice

Cold Brew

100g coarse coffee + 1000g water

Steep 12–18 hours, strain and serve over ice.

Dirty Latte

Cold cup + 120ml cold milk + freshly extracted espresso layered on top.

⭐ Special Brews

Hojicha Latte

Hot: 5g Hojicha powder whisk with warm water + sweetener (optional) + 200ml steamed milk

Ice: 5g Hojicha powder whisk with warm water + sweetener (optional) + 120ml cold milk + ice

Affogato

Vanilla ice cream topped with hot single espresso.

Yuzu Sparkling Coffee

2 scoop yuzu puree + sparkling water + espresso over ice.
